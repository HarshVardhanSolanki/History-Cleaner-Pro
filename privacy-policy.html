Privacy Policy

Extension Name: History Cleaner Pro

Developer: Harsh Vardhan Solanki

Effective Date: February 18, 2026

1. Overview

History Cleaner Pro is a Chrome extension designed to allow users to search and bulk delete selected entries from their browsing history. This extension operates entirely within the user's browser.

2. Data Collection

History Cleaner Pro does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No browsing data is sent to external servers.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No personal information is collected.
  • No account registration is required.

3. Use of Permissions

The extension requests the "history" permission solely to:

  • Search the user’s browsing history based on a keyword.
  • Delete history entries explicitly selected by the user.

All actions occur locally within the browser. The extension does not access passwords, form data, cookies, or any other sensitive information.

4. Data Storage

History Cleaner Pro does not store user data on any external servers. Any processing occurs locally within the Chrome browser.

5. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use third-party services, APIs, advertising networks, or analytics providers.

6. Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no external data exposure. All operations are executed using Chrome’s official extension APIs.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the extension’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
